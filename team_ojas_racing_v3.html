Who We Are

Born To Build.
Race. Win.

Redefining the extent of ambition forged through engineering, Team Ojas Racing represents VIT Vellore on the world stage, building race cars that go toe-to-toe with the finest institutions worldwide, proving that the relentless pursuit of excellence knows no boundaries. Across ten seasons of international competition, we have upheld a singular standard: that excellence in electric motorsport demands no less than total ownership of the craft and an audacious maverick spirit.
