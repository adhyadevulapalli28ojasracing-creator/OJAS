The Story
Seven Seasons.
One Mission.

Team Ojas Racing is VIT Vellore's Formula Student Electric team — a group of engineers, designers and managers united by one goal: build the fastest, smartest electric race car we can, then take it to the world.

Founded in 2012, we have grown from a handful of students building in a workshop to a 80+ member organisation competing on the international stage at Formula Student Germany and Formula Student Austria.

Every component on our car is engineered, simulated, manufactured and tested by students. No outsourcing. No shortcuts. Pure engineering.

01
Engineering First
We believe in rigorous simulation before fabrication. Every subsystem goes through FEA, CFD or HIL testing before a component is manufactured — no guessing, only data.
02
Student-Led, Industry-Grade
From a custom-wound motor to a bespoke accumulator management system — everything is student-designed to industry-grade standards and scrutinised by international judges.
03
Global Ambition
Competing at FSG and FSA means going head-to-head with top European universities. We don't just show up — we show up prepared to fight for every point on the scoreboard.
04
The People Behind the Car
80+ engineers across powertrain, aero, chassis, electronics and operations. Alumni of Ojas go on to Mercedes, Bosch, Tesla, Tata Motors, Mahindra Racing and more.
